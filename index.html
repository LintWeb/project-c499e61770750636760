About GnG Makeup Store

Your Local Beauty Sanctuary in Chattogram

Founded with a passion for high-quality cosmetics, GnG Makeup Store has become a trusted destination for beauty enthusiasts in Bangladesh. Located in the heart of Chattogram at Blossom Garden Market, we take pride in offering authentic products that help you express your unique style.


Our collection is carefully curated to include everything from professional-grade makeup to nourishing skincare, ensuring that every customer finds exactly what they need to feel confident and beautiful.
